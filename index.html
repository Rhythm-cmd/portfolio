About me

Ankit Chawda is a popular Anchor, Host, Radio Jockey, Entertainer, and EMCEE based in Mandsaur city, Madhya Pradesh, India. With over 10 years of industry experience, he has hosted more than 2000 national events, including weddings, conferences, product launches, carnivals, cultural events, concerts, anniversaries, birthdays, corporate shows, fashion shows, school/college functions, political events, sports events, university gigs, and many more.

He also plays the different comic characters of TV and bollywood like Dr.Gulati, Chulbul Pandey and PK. Ankit Anchor is becoming the rising star of Central India.
